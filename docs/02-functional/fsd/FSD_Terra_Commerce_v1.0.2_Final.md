## Normative Statement

All requirements, rules, scope, actors, non-functional requirements, and acceptance traceability in FSD v1.0.1 are approved and incorporated into this Final baseline.

The former open-decision register is closed by ADR-0008 through ADR-0022. Those ADRs are authoritative where v1.0.1 described a topic as undecided.

## Approval Closure

- Product behavior is approved.
- Technical feasibility is confirmed by the completed architecture, state, data, contract, security, technical-design, quality, infrastructure, and operations reviews.
- ADR owners are assigned.
- All 18 PRD acceptance criteria remain covered.

Frontend, identity/session, tenant routing, payments, delivery credentials, object storage, email, reservation expiry, order numbering, tax, returns/refunds, plan lifecycle, customer identity, Medusa extensions, and PostgreSQL implementation conventions are fixed by ADR-0008 through ADR-0022.
